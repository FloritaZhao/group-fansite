Moussaka

A hearty baked dish made with layers of eggplant, minced meat, and creamy béchamel sauce. Often called “the Greek lasagna,” it warms both the belly and the soul.

Souvlaki

These grilled meat skewers are a street food favorite. Served with pita, tzatziki, and lemon, they’re simple, flavorful, and perfect for late-night beach strolls.

Spanakopita

A flaky filo pastry filled with spinach, feta cheese, and herbs. Crunchy on the outside, savory on the inside — perfect for picnics under olive trees.
